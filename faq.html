FAQ

frequently asked question

Are your products of high quality and reliable?

Yes, at MEDINST, we prioritize quality and reliability. We partner with renowned manufacturers and suppliers who adhere to stringent quality standards. Our products undergo rigorous testing to ensure they meet industry regulations and deliver exceptional performance.

Can you provide product demonstrations?

Yes, we can arrange product demonstrations upon request. Please contact us to schedule a demonstration at your convenience.

Can you provide technical support and assistance?

Yes, MEDINST understands that each healthcare facility may have specific needs. We offer customized solutions tailored to your unique requirements. Our team will work closely with you to understand your needs and provide personalized recommendations.

Where does MEDINST operate and ship its products?

MEDINST operates in Indore and serves clients locally as well as nationwide. We ship our products across India, ensuring timely delivery to your desired location.

How long does it take to receive a response from MEDINST?

We strive to respond to your queries as quickly as possible. You can expect a prompt response within our business hours.

What types of medical instruments does MEDINST supply?

MEDINST supplies a wide range of medical instruments, including multi-para monitors, ECGs, ventilators, oxygen concentrators, bi-pap machines, syringe pumps, and storage solutions. Our comprehensive product catalog caters to various healthcare needs.
